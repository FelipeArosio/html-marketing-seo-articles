Black Friday Champagne Offers: how to serve and pair discounted sparkling wines

To truly enhance the Black Friday Champagne offers, it is important to think not only about the price but also about how the bottles will be served. A correct temperature, around 8–10 °C for Brut Champagnes like Cuvée D and slightly higher for the D Millésimé 2009, allows the aromas to open without losing freshness.

Regarding glasses, tulip-shaped glasses are often preferable to very narrow flutes: they allow the aromas to rise and keep the mousse lively. The Cuvée D Brut is well suited for aperitifs and seafood or land appetizers, while the D Rosé finds its ideal dimension with dishes based on delicate cured meats, flavorful fish, or dishes with light spicy notes.

The Champagne D Millésimé 2009, thanks to its important structure and notes of dried fruit, citrus, and brioche, can accompany more complex dishes, up to a structured fish main course or white meat preparations. Thinking about these pairings in advance allows you to better exploit the Champagne offers, transforming every bottle purchased during Black Friday into a reasoned tasting moment.
