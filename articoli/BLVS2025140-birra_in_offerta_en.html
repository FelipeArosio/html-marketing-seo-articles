La Clàsica Er Boquerón: craft beer made with sea water

La Clàsica by Er Boquerón is a craft beer produced using sea water from selected areas of the Mediterranean Sea, characterised by clear waters rich in marine flora. The recipe is based on a few essential ingredients: water, barley malt, hops, yeast and sea water. The beer is neither filtered nor pasteurised and undergoes double fermentation, with the second fermentation taking place in the bottle and creating a natural yeast sediment. In the glass it appears coppery, with orange highlights and fine bubbles; on the nose, notes of malt and biscuit emerge, together with iodised aromas, hints of saffron, dried grass and ripe fruit. On the palate, balanced salinity and balsamic notes reminiscent of liquorice stand out, making it a truly unique Golden Ale.
