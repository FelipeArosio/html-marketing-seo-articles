Where Offida Docg is produced within Marche wines

The versions of Offida Docg within Marche wines

Entering the Offida Docg denomination means encountering three distinct paths. Offida Pecorino enhances a native white grape variety, Offida Passerina focuses on freshness and drinkability, while Offida Rosso is based on Montepulciano. The production regulations require at least 85% of the main grape variety for each type, with a maximum of 15% of other non-aromatic grape varieties suitable for cultivation in Marche.
